"Elevate is a podcast hosted by students under the Multimedia Arts program of Mapua Malayan Colleges Mindanao. The podcast features some of the creative minds of Davao City and serve as a medium for these artistic personalities to express their passion for the art. The event aims to allow these featured artists to express their knowledge about their craft and share their thoughts about working in the industry they are in.
